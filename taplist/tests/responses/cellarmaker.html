Cellarmaker Brewing – Our Tasting Room

Our tasting room is custom built with the help of many local vendors. Come enjoy its relaxing atmosphere. Beers are available in tasters, half pints, pints, as well as 1 Liter and 64oz growlers to go. Friendly reminder, we are a 21+ establishment. Cheers!

Triple Berry Berliner on tap!

Posted on February 1, 2014 in News

Hey guys, we are tapping our Triple Berry Berliner today. It’s a lightly tart Berliner Weiss style ale to which we added raspberry, blueberry and blackberry puree. The berry aromas and flavors are nicely balanced with the pilsner and wheat malts. This ale is quite drinkable, but you might not want to because you’ll be too busy looking at its striking purple color! Cheers!
