Reservations
We do not take reservations except for our beer dinner events. Our host runs the wait list when we are full in the evening, and will accept a cellphone number and text you when your table is ready. We do not accept names for the wait list over the phone.
Private Parties

Mikkeller Bar is pleased to offer its private cellar room to interested parties for private events / structured beer tastings.

Total capacity is limited to 20 people.

For booking, or more information please contact Bob DeMoisey via email, bob@mikkellerbar.com.
