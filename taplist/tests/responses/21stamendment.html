The Restaurant

The 21st Amendment is a full service restaurant with bar open seven days a week for lunch and dinner and Sunday brunch. We are located in the SOMA area of San Francisco, just two blocks from the San Francisco Giants park. We serve casual grilled cuisine and our handcrafted beers and sodas.
